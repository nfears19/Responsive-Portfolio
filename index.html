About Me

Nakiyah Fears is an advertiser and writer from Cleveland, Ohio, in love with photography, design, and music. She seeks ways to challenge herself while gaining new skills and knowledge. Nakiyah graduated from Kent State University with a degree in Advertising and a minor in Writing. Her passion for writing developed in her early childhood from checking out a lot of books from the library and being assigned creative writing prompts during class throughout middle school. Nakiyah comes from a family of artistic and entrepreneurial individuals, so creativity flows through her flood and drives her to work with a purpose.


She is currently a student in the Case Western Reserve University’s Coding Bootcamp to begin her journey as a software developer, both deepening and broadening her expertise. In college, she enjoyed designing presentations, pitch decks, print advertisements, and social media posts for college assignments or entering in design contests for student organizations. When she’s not spending her downtime writing her book, she’ll find freelance projects, create spec ads, or blog on her website.
